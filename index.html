Tribute to Jaco Pastorius

Jaco Pastorius playing his '62 Fender Jazz Bass

Jaco Pastorius (real name John Francis Anthony Pastorius III, December 1, 1951 - September 21, 1987) was an American virtuoso electric bass palyer. Originally a drummer, following in the footsteps of his father, stand-up drummer Jack Pastorius, "Jaco" switched to bass at age 15.
