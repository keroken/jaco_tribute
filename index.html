Tribute to Jaco Pastorius

Jaco Pastorius playing his '62 Fender Jazz Bass

Jaco Pastorius (real name John Francis Anthony Pastorius III, December 1, 1951 - September 21, 1987) was an American jazz bassist who was a member of Weather Report from 1976 to 1981. He worked with Pat Metheny, Joni Mitchell, and recorded albums as a solo artist and band leader. His bass playing employed funk, lyrical solos, bass chords, and innovative harmonics. He was one of seven bassists to be inducted into the DownBeat Jazz Hall of Fame.
